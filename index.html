Skanda: The Attacker

Rayudu is the Chief Minister of Andhra Pradesh who arranges his daughter's
marriage. During the marriage, the son of Chief Minister of Telangana Ranjith Reddy arrives
at the marriage venue as a guest and elopes with her to everybody's shock. This angers both the Chief Ministers and they turn
into bitter foes from friends. Meanwhile, Bhaskar Raju is a college student who kidnaps
Rayudu's daughter and Ranjith Reddy's daughter, Sreeleela Reddy, who also happens to be his classmate.
It is revealed that kidnappings are connected to the CEO of the Crown Group
Company Rudrakanti Ramakrishna Raju, who is awaiting his death penalty
and was imprisoned falsely by both Ranjith Reddy and Rayudu for refusing to legitimize
their illegal money. It is also revealed that Ramakrishna Raju is actually the
best friend of Bhaskar's father Manikanta Raju. Bhaskar wants
to prove Ramakrishna Raju's innocence as he was forced to
take the blame in order to protect his daughter Rudraganti Parineeta from harm as
she was held captive by Ranjith and Rayudu's men.
With no choice, Ranjith and Rayudu bails out Ramakrishna Raju from prison.
Following this, the duo attack Bhaskar and his family, but they are
saved by Bhaskar's doppelgänger Skanda. Eventually, Ranjith and Rayudu confess their crimes to
the media. When Ramakrishna Raju inquires about Skanda,
it is revealed that Skanda is a notorious assassin from Morocco
who is responsible for the death of Morocco-based powerful crime boss
Daniel. After learning about his history, Rayudu
and Ranjith Reddy becomes frightened of him as he walks away smiling,
indicating that his story will unfold in the
sequel titled Skanda 2.[10]
